# Meridian Prime Species Guide

> Fictional worldbuilding reference for the Meridian Prime universe.

This document defines the canonical visual, anatomical, cultural, and design language for the major peoples of Meridian Prime, Aeonos, and àæonos. It is intended for novels, comics, visual development, tabletop materials, and 16-bit game adaptation.

---

## Aeonolacertians / Saurasapiens

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

**Species Type:** hybrid saurian people native to Matter Aeonos.

**Formal Species / Civilization Name:** Aeonolacertian.

**Common Umbrella Field Term:** Saurasapien.

**Core Visual Principle:** Aeonolacertians are a crossbred saurian civilization with mostly humanoid body plans, expressive saurian features, universal tails, vivid color palettes, and morph-type traits that express across mixed populations. They are not simple dinosaur people, generic lizardfolk, or humans wearing reptile masks.

Aeonolacertians should look ancient, alive, intelligent, physically powerful, culturally complex, and technologically mature. Their bodies and clothing should communicate heat, pressure, motion, brass, copper, color, class-line heritage, and survivor history.

---

## Aeonolacertian Baseline Body Plan

Aeonolacertians stand upright, walk bipedally, use tools, wear clothing and gear, and express emotion through face, posture, tail, crest, frill, quill, and eye movement.

### Shared Anatomical Traits

- Mostly humanoid torso proportions with saurian musculature and posture.
- Universal tails used for balance, communication, combat posture, and emotional expression.
- Four-fingered human-like hands with claws and morph-type shape variations.
- Four-toed human-like feet with claws and morph-type shape variations.
- Saurian heads, jaws, brows, crests, frills, spines, quills, and snouts depending on morph-type.
- Almost vestigial lips: enough soft tissue for expression and some speech shaping, but not fully mammalian lips.
- Expressive faces capable of warmth, suspicion, grief, humor, command, and fear without becoming human faces pasted onto reptiles.
- Scaled skin, usually with class-associated vivid color palettes.
- Feathers, quills, filaments, crests, frills, or spines present in most lineages, though expression varies by morph-type.

### Hands and Feet

**Hands:** four fingers, including an opposable or semi-opposable thumb equivalent, with claws. The exact claw curvature, finger length, palm shape, and knuckle structure vary by morph-type.

**Feet:** four toes, broad or narrow depending on morph-type, with claws. They may be booted, armored, wrapped, or partly visible depending on dress.

Design note: avoid giving Aeonolacertians ordinary human hands or feet with scales painted over them. Their anatomy should feel adapted to tools but not mammalian.

## Aeonolacertian Population Structure and Morph Types

Aeonolacertians are mixed populations with recognizable morph-type tendencies rather than rigid biological castes. Morph types often correlate with specialized roles, inherited family traditions, military functions, ritual duties, and cultural expectations, but individuals may cross boundaries. After the Catastrophic Interaction, survival pressures have blurred some older distinctions.

A city, enclave, or faction should show mixed Aeonolacertian populations: different morph-types living, working, arguing, fighting, raising young, and maintaining machinery together.

### Naming Systems

**Aeonolacertian** is the formal species and civilization name.

**Saurasapien** is the common umbrella field term for Aeonolacertian morph-type people. It is most often used by Meridians, field personnel, artists, scout teams, mechanics, and game/comic adaptation notes when referring to the five recognizable `-sapien` morph-type expressions as a group.

The term **Saurasapien** does not replace Aeonolacertian in formal canon. It is a practical human-facing umbrella label for the visible morph-type system.

| Archival Morph Label | Common Field Name | Typical Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Velociraptoroid | Velocisapien | speed, scouting, rapid movement |
| Tyrannisauroid | Tyrannosapien | command, heavy war, force projection |
| Bajadasauroid | Bajadrasapien | signal, ritual science, resonance |
| Ankylosauroid | Ankylosapien | defense, engineering, endurance |
| Ceratopsinoid | Cerasapien | civic order, guardianship, family law |

Example usage:

- **World Works archive:** “Subject exhibits Tyrannisauroid-dominant morphology.”
- **Meridian mechanic:** “That’s Tyrannosapien pressure gear. Don’t touch it unless you want your arm educated.”
- **Field team note:** “The commission included one Saurasapien of each major field-recognized morph type.”

## Aeonolacertian Color, Ornamentation, Clothing, and Armor

Aeonolacertians use vivid colors. Their class-lines are color-associated, though exact palettes vary by region, family, age, role, and ritual context. Scale color, feather coloration, quill dye, armor paint, copper ornament, and ritual markings can all signal role, faction, ancestry, grief, readiness for war, or oath-status.

### Material Palette

- brass
- copper
- oxidized copper
- dark iron
- pressure-treated leather
- colored scale paint
- polished stone
- red crystal for elite Heart Guard armor
- steam-darkened cloth
- field gear and adventure wear

### Scale and Armor Painting

Aeonolacertians paint scales, armor, crests, frills, and quills for war, mourning, ritual, memory rites, class-line ceremonies, siege defense, and faction identity.

Paint may mark heat-readiness, unit identity, oath of defense, mourning for the Thousandfold Rupture, Central Boiler sanctum duty, Bellows Rite participation, or Heart Guard final-stand status.

### Civilian Clothing

Civilians wear adventure clothes and practical gear: rugged jackets, belts, pouches, harnesses, tool rolls, heat-lined wraps, copper clasps, brass utility buckles, traveling cloaks, pressure-safe boots or wraps, goggles, scale-safe layers, and tail-compatible packs.

### Steam-Oracle Clothing

Steam-Oracles wear robes over functional adventure gear. Their robes should feel like practical ritual-engineering garments layered over field gear, tool harnesses, heat-safe clothing, goggles, lenses, tuning instruments, and portable gauges.

### Aeolipile Clothing

The Aeolipile wear steampunk adventure wear and gear: compact pressure packs, rotating brass devices, throat-resonance collars, breath-control gear, heat-lined coats, reinforced gloves, acoustic tools, valve keys, and portable Bellows Rite instruments.

### Infantry Armor

Aeonolacertian infantry wear steam automaton suits: brass and dark iron exosuit components, steam-circulating tubes, pressure gauges, heat vents, shield-lock ports, reinforced tail protection, boiler-linked backpacks, and armored throat/chest protection.

### Heart Guard Armor

Heart Guard armor is red crystal armor. It is elite, sacred, dangerous, and built for last-stand defense of the Mother Heart. It is designed to survive or channel extreme **20 K’al** conditions during hyper-metabolic combat states.

## Saurasapien Morph-Type Visual Guide

**Role Tendencies:** scouts, messengers, tactical intelligence, rapid-strike defense, infiltration, memory-runners.

**Body Shape:** lean, agile, narrow-waisted, quick-limbed, light but strong.

**Visual Notes:** narrow saurian skull, alert eyes, sharp expressive brows, longer fingers/toes, curved claws, flexible balancing tail, common feathers/quills along forearms, head crest, tail ridge, neck, or shoulders.

---

### Tyrannisauroid / Tyrannosapien

**Role Tendencies:** command, heavy war leadership, territorial defense, executive authority, Heart Guard candidates, ceremonial force.

**Body Shape:** large, powerful, deep-chested, thick-necked, heavy-jawed, imposing.

**Visual Notes:** broad skull, strong brow, deep jaw, robust four-finger hands and four-toed feet, thick stabilizing tail, heavy crest-like or mane-like quill/feather expression.

**Color Association:** reds, deep oranges, black striping, iron-dark markings, heat-associated war colors.

---

### Bajadasauroid / Bajadrasapien

**Role Tendencies:** signal communication, ritual science, environmental interpretation, diplomacy, memory traditions, pressure resonance.

**Body Shape:** taller or more elongated than many morphs, with elegant neck and prominent dorsal or neck spines.

**Visual Notes:** elongated facial structure, expressive eyes, graceful crests/spines, long expressive tail, quills along crest, spine, neck, tail, or shoulders, often ornamented with copper rings or ritual paint.

**Color Association:** blues, violets, copper-greens, oxidized copper tones, signal-bright accents.

---

### Ankylosauroid / Ankylosapien

**Role Tendencies:** protection, engineering, fortress-building, infrastructure, endurance, defensive warfare.

**Body Shape:** broad, sturdy, low-centered, heavily built, armored-looking even without armor.

**Visual Notes:** reinforced skull shapes, heavy brows, broad hands/feet, thick tail that may show club-like traits, bristle-like quills or feathers between osteoderms and armor plates.

**Color Association:** ochre, rust, bronze, dark green, slate, blackened copper, defensive markings.

---

### Ceratopsinoid / Cerasapien

**Role Tendencies:** civic order, elder councils, community defense, family structures, agriculture, law, ceremonial guardianship.

**Body Shape:** strong, grounded, broad-shouldered, dignified.

**Visual Notes:** horned or frilled facial structure, stable four-finger hands and four-toed feet, strong tail, frills often painted or ornamented, feathers/quills along frill edges, neck, shoulders, or tail.

**Color Association:** golds, creams, deep greens, red-browns, white ritual markings, civic color bands.

## Aeonolacertian Expression and Design Rules

Aeonolacertians are emotionally readable through eye shape, brow plates, jaw angle, vestigial lip movement, nostril flare, crest lift, frill angle, quill bristling, tail position, claw tapping, throat rumble, posture, and heat-state.

### Artist and Adaptation Rules

1. Aeonolacertians are mostly humanoid with saurian features, not humans in masks and not ordinary dinosaurs walking upright.
2. Tails are universal.
3. Hands have four fingers with claws; feet have four toes with claws.
4. Faces are expressive, with almost vestigial lips.
5. Feathers, quills, crests, frills, and spines are common in most lineages.
6. Vivid colors are canon.
7. Class-lines are color-associated but not color-locked.
8. Brass, copper, oxidized copper, and pressure-machine ornamentation belong strongly to their culture.
9. Civilians look adventurous and practical, not medieval peasants.
10. Steam-Oracles wear robes over functional adventure gear.
11. Aeolipile dress like steampunk explorer-engineers.
12. Infantry wear steam automaton suits.
13. Heart Guard wear red crystal armor.
14. Scale and armor painting for war, ritual, grief, and oath-status is canon.
15. Morph types are mixed population traits associated with specialized roles, not separate species.
16. `-sauroid` terms are archival or academic labels; `-sapien` terms are common field names.
17. **Saurasapien** is the common umbrella field term for Aeonolacertian morph-type people.
18. **Aeonolacertian** remains the formal species and civilization name.

## áæbsconditians / Absconditians / Antisapiens

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

**Species Type:** blue-skinned anti-matter humanoid people native to àæonos.

**Formal Civilization / Species Name:** áæbsconditian.

**Simplified Human / Common Name:** Absconditian.

**Common Umbrella Field Term:** Antisapien.

**Plural Field Term:** Antisapiens.

**Core Visual Principle:** Antisapiens are áæbsconditian phenotype people whose visible differences are primarily expressed through signal behavior, glow patterning, stealth adaptation, resonance markers, identity markings, bio-cabling, technology access, signal aesthetics, ritual styling, and anti-matter biology rather than body-shape ecology.

Antisapiens should look elegant, unsettling, alive, intelligent, blue-skinned, cyber-resonant, and profoundly alien without becoming generic fantasy creatures.

## Antisapien Baseline Body Plan

Antisapiens are blue-skinned humanoids with anti-matter biological and cyber-resonant traits.

Their bodies are generally humanoid in silhouette, but their visual identity comes from their eyes, glow behavior, signal markings, posture, stealth adaptation, lattice ornamentation, bio-cabling, technology access, signal aesthetic logic, ritual makeup, and how their presence interacts with light and perception.

### Shared Anatomical and Visual Traits

- Blue-skinned humanoid body plan.
- Black irises.
- Magenta pupils.
- Teal tapetum lucidum, often visible as low-light eye-shine.
- Smooth, living skin.
- Rounded or humanlike ears rather than pointed fantasy ears.
- Signal-band markings, glow asymmetries, lattice patterns, bio-cabling, signal aesthetics, or resonance traces depending on phenotype.
- Cyberpunk / anti-matter aesthetic language: black glass, magenta or amaranth light, teal optics, neural lattice ornamentation, stealth surfaces, and signal interfaces.

Antisapiens are living anti-matter people. Their anti-life nature is cosmological, biological, technological, and philosophical, not a decay or fantasy-monster shortcut.

## Antisapien Bio-Cabling / Hair

Antisapien “hair” is not ordinary mammalian hair. It is **bio-cabling**: a living signal-organ expression tied to anti-matter biology, cyber-resonance, signal-band phenotype, identity, and social function.

**Antisapien bio-cabling varies by signal-band. It may be absent, hidden, luminous, bundled, or exposed depending on phenotype.**

### Nullsapien Bio-Cabling

**Expression:** absent or fully internalized.

**Visual:** bald, smooth scalp, severe symmetry.

**Meaning:** wireless signal control, sealed sovereignty, disciplined command.

**Rule:** Nullsapiens should generally be bald because they represent wireless technology and contained signal authority.

### Veilsapien Bio-Cabling

**Expression:** retractable dark fiber optics.

**Visual:** matte-black or midnight-blue strands with minimal reflectivity, able to flatten, retract, dim, or vanish into the silhouette.

**Meaning:** stealth, suppressed signal, covert observation, phase discipline.

**Rule:** from a distance, Veilsapien cabling may make them appear almost bald until light catches the strands.

### Amaranthsapien Bio-Cabling

**Expression:** luminous fiber optics with glowing tips.

**Visual:** magenta, amaranth, violet, teal, or electric-blue fiber-optic strands, often braided, looped, or arranged in harmonic patterns.

**Meaning:** resonance science, signal theory, dream-contact, harmonic computation, Madrigalist practice.

**Rule:** glowing tips may brighten during song, signal reading, dream-contact, or interface work.

### Vaultsapien Bio-Cabling

**Expression:** organized network-cable bundles.

**Visual:** the strongest current canon is **shiny black network-cable bio-cabling with a subtle blue shimmer**. Cobalt, teal, graphite, silver-gray, or muted magenta accents remain valid as highlights, clasps, tags, interface sheathing, or ceremonial bindings.

**Meaning:** identity preservation, genealogies, survivor ledgers, continuity custody, archival discipline.

**Rule:** Vaultsapien cabling should be glossy black, blue-shimmered, organized, heavy, elegant, industrial, and ceremonial. It may be bound with identity clasps, memory tags, black-glass connectors, record seals, or continuity ornaments. It should not look like ordinary dreadlocks or literal office ethernet cable.

### Lividisapien Bio-Cabling

**Expression:** exposed multicolored phone-wire hair.

**Visual:** visible internal cable-like strands in magenta, teal, yellow, orange, violet, pale blue, white, and other signal colors. Stringy phone-wire mohawks are common.

**Meaning:** contradiction reading, suppressed history, Split Signal analysis, revisionist truth-seeking.

**Rule:** Lividisapien cabling may look tangled, frayed, asymmetrical, patched, or mohawked, but it should still feel meaningful and beautiful rather than random.

### Bio-Cabling Design Balance

- **Nullsapien** = wireless / internal signal.
- **Veilsapien** = retractable dark fiber optics / hidden signal.
- **Amaranthsapien** = glowing fiber optics / harmonic signal.
- **Vaultsapien** = glossy black blue-shimmer network-cable bundles / archived signal.
- **Lividisapien** = exposed phone wires / contradictory signal.

Bio-cabling can move, glow, dim, retract, pulse, or respond to interface states depending on band. It should feel biological and technological at once.

## Antisapien Signal-Band Technology Access

Each Antisapien signal-band is bound by a technology-access ceiling. This access is biological, cultural, political, and infrastructural. It reflects what systems their signal-band can safely interface with, what systems society permits them to operate, and what kinds of technology their bio-cabling and signal behavior are naturally adapted to.

**Each Antisapien signal-band can access its own technology tier and all lesser tiers. Higher tiers require special permission, restricted bypass, rare talent, ritual modification, or dangerous interface corruption.**

### Tier 5: Nullsapien

Nullsapiens have access to AI systems, sovereign cognition systems, predictive governance engines, command intelligence, high-order automated decision networks, and all lesser forms of Antisapien technology. This matches their bald/internalized bio-cabling and wireless signal authority.

### Tier 4: Veilsapien

Veilsapiens have access to AR systems, perception-layer overlays, stealth-field interfaces, phase-navigation displays, visual misdirection networks, VR systems, archive mesh systems, smart tech, and legacy systems. This matches their retractable dark fiber-optic bio-cabling and stealth role.

### Tier 3: Amaranthsapien

Amaranthsapiens have access to VR systems, resonance chambers, dream-contact systems, harmonic simulations, immersive memory theaters, virtual signal laboratories, archive mesh systems, smart tech, and legacy systems. This matches their luminous fiber-optic bio-cabling with glowing tips.

### Tier 2: Vaultsapien

Vaultsapiens have access to intranet systems, the Vault Mesh, archive mesh systems, identity vaults, continuity ledgers, survivor records, population memory systems, smart tech, and legacy systems.

**Vault Mesh** is the preferred term for the secured áæbsconditian intranet used for identity records, survivor ledgers, population continuity, official history, memory proofs, and protected internal archives.

### Tier 1: Lividisapien

Lividisapiens have access to smart tech, old devices, phreak-culture systems, counter-system tools, signal salvage, legacy interfaces, discarded hardware, corrupted devices, unofficial workarounds, exposed wiring, and forgotten infrastructure.

**Wire access** and **fringe access** are common terms for Lividisapien interaction with discarded, outdated, unofficial, corrupted, or physically exposed systems.

## Antisapien Signal Aesthetic Logic

Each Antisapien signal-band has an associated musical genre logic. This does not mean every individual literally performs or belongs to that music scene. Instead, the genre acts as a visual, cultural, tonal, and movement-design anchor for artists, writers, game designers, and adaptation teams.

**Antisapien signal-bands are partly readable through genre-coded signal aesthetics: clothing, posture, lighting, technology, movement, bio-cabling, ritual styling, and social behavior should all carry the visual rhythm of the band’s genre.**

### Nullsapien = Ambient

Nullsapiens are associated with **ambient** aesthetics: stillness, silence as power, invisible structure, atmosphere over performance, wireless authority, controlled space, minimal movement, AI sovereignty, black-glass calm, and quiet pressure.

Nullsapiens should feel like the sound in the walls: present everywhere, rarely visible, and impossible to ignore once noticed.

### Veilsapien = Underground Hip Hop

Veilsapiens are associated with **underground hip hop** aesthetics: street intelligence, urban movement, coded language, rhythm and flow, tactical improvisation, low-profile swagger, hidden knowledge, field adaptability, AR perception layers, hoodie/mask traversal gear, and rugged streetwise faces.

Veilsapiens should feel like stealth with a beat: covert, mobile, observant, and capable of moving through Meridian Prime without immediately alerting the general public.

### Amaranthsapien = Dungeonsynth

Amaranthsapiens are associated with **dungeonsynth** aesthetics: ritual atmosphere, synthetic mysticism, ancient-future mood, dream-space architecture, ceremonial solitude, VR cathedrals, luminous fiberoptic bio-cabling, robed technologists, memory-space exploration, harmonic imagination, white ritual face paint, and black visor-drip makeup.

Amaranthsapiens should feel like resonance scholars inside a lost digital cathedral: ceremonial, immersive, synthetic, ancient, and strange.

### Vaultsapien = Industrial

Vaultsapiens are associated with **industrial** aesthetics: machinery, repetition, secured systems, locked rhythm, archive infrastructure, ordered pressure, continuity through mechanism, glossy black network-cable bio-cabling, identity as machinery, records as hard systems, precise archive makeup, and formal facial-hair styling in male presentation.

Vaultsapiens should feel like the archive is a machine and they are one of its living components.

### Lividisapien = Punk

Lividisapiens are associated with **punk** aesthetics: contradiction, rebellion, body modification, anti-authority style, raw truth, signal damage, exposed wiring, patched identity, low-clearance survival, phone-wire mohawks, phreak-culture tools, and noise as truth.

Lividisapiens should feel like truth screaming through bad wiring.

### Signal Aesthetic Summary

| Signal-Band | Signal Aesthetic | Design Meaning |
|---|---|---|
| Nullsapien | Ambient | hidden control, wireless authority, AI sovereignty |
| Veilsapien | Underground hip hop | street stealth, coded movement, AR field adaptation |
| Amaranthsapien | Dungeonsynth | VR ritual, dream-interface, synthetic mysticism |
| Vaultsapien | Industrial | archive machinery, identity systems, continuity infrastructure |
| Lividisapien | Punk | contradiction, phreak culture, recovered truth, exposed wires |

### Signal Aesthetic Design Balance

- **Nullsapien** = silence as control.
- **Veilsapien** = rhythm as concealment.
- **Amaranthsapien** = harmony as interface.
- **Vaultsapien** = sequence as memory.
- **Lividisapien** = noise as truth.

### Movement-Language Guidance

- Nullsapiens move minimally, with ambient stillness and sovereign restraint.
- Veilsapiens move rhythmically, efficiently, and street-smart.
- Amaranthsapiens move ceremonially, as if interfacing with invisible dream architecture.
- Vaultsapiens move deliberately, precisely, and mechanically.
- Lividisapiens move defiantly, irregularly, and expressively.

## Antisapien Ritual Makeup and Cultural Styling

Antisapien ritual makeup is cultural, ceremonial, and signal-aesthetic. It is not a biological sign of death, decay, or monsterhood. Antisapiens remain living blue-skinned anti-matter people.

### Amaranthsapien Ritual Makeup

Amaranthsapiens may wear **corpse-paint-style ritual makeup** as part of resonance rites, dream-contact ceremonies, Madrigalist practice, and dungeonsynth-coded signal aesthetics.

Common traits:

- pale white or bone-white ritual makeup across the face
- black makeup beneath or around the VR visor
- black streaks or drips descending from beneath the VR goggles or visor
- darkened lips or thin black mouth markings
- geometric or lattice-like ritual marks at the forehead, temples, throat, hands, or collarbone
- makeup integrated with glowing fiberoptic bio-cabling and VR interface states

Canon meaning:

- white ritual makeup = ceremonial detachment from ordinary sensory reality
- black drips beneath the visor = dream-contact bleed, signal grief, or immersion residue
- dark markings around the mouth = harmonic silence, choral restraint, or Madrigalist concentration
- makeup reinforces the Amaranthsapien signal aesthetic: **Dungeonsynth**
- makeup should feel like cybernetic ritual scholarship, not horror shorthand

### Vaultsapien Ritual Makeup

Vaultsapiens may wear **restrained corpse-paint-style ritual makeup** during archive ceremonies, lineage verification, continuity trials, Vault Mesh audits, or identity rites.

Common traits:

- restrained pale or white facial panels
- black vertical marks around the eyes, forehead, or bridge of the nose
- precise symmetrical streaks rather than messy drips
- archive glyphs or small crossline markings
- subtle black accents that reinforce industrial severity
- makeup should look ordered, audited, and ceremonial

Canon meaning:

- white makeup = archival neutrality, identity custody, or ritual severance from personal bias
- black marks = record weight, continuity burden, or sealed testimony
- precise facial markings = sequence as memory
- makeup reinforces the Vaultsapien signal aesthetic: **Industrial**

Unlike Amaranthsapien makeup, which may drip and flow like dream-contact residue, Vaultsapien makeup should be precise, symmetrical, and industrial.
