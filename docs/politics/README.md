## Major Political Factions / Polities

### The Bought Nation

File:

`docs/politics/the-bought-nation.md`

Canon status:

**MAIN CANON, EXPANDABLE**

Summary:

The **Bought Nation** is the forgotten migrant human polity of the Meridian Prime Escarpment / Prime Plateau settlement sphere: a people purchased into survival by World Works, maintained by Prime Ops, worked by NCI and contractor houses, and still trying to become a nation no ledger can own.

Key canon anchors:

- formal World Works charter language: **Meridian Human Settlement Compact**
- NCI / route-family term: **Bought Folk**
- Hydropolis interpretation: **The Leased People**
- Craton interpretation: **The Nation Without Its Own Gate**
- central motto: **Earth forgot us. World Works counted us. We remembered ourselves.**
- political wound: legal entanglement, inherited lease dependency, and sovereignty without true gate control
- capital / staging city: **Charterhold**
- working edge / border logging town: **Ledger Falls**

Canon principle:

**The Bought Nation is not poor because it lacks a nation. It is poor because it has had to build one inside someone else's receipt book.**

### Reclaimers

File:

`docs/politics/the-bought-nation.md`

Canon status:

**MAIN CANON FACTION SEED, EXPANDABLE**

Summary:

The **Reclaimers** are a Bought Nation political faction seeking true land ownership, treaty recognition, independent route authority, and an end to corporate settlement dependency.

Key canon anchors:

- World Works calls them destabilizers
- Prime Ops may call them a route-security concern
- NCI families may be divided over them
- Reclaimer activity is visible in Charterhold's Reclaimer Row and around Ledger Falls / Green Line disputes
- exact leadership and organizational structure remain open

Guardrail:

Do not make the Reclaimers automatically heroic, terrorist, or unified. They should remain politically varied and expandable.

---

### The Free Scabs

File:

`docs/politics/the-free-scabs.md`

Canon status:

**MAIN CANON, EXPANDABLE**

Summary:

The **Free Scabs** are a seasonal scabland / basin outlaw compact made from defectors, fugitives, ruined contractors, Hydropolis exiles, Craton oath-breakers, Bought Nation runaways, World Works deserters, and Traverse ghosts.

Key canon anchors:

- formal outlaw-polity name: **The Free Scab Compact**
- hidden homeland / base-system: **The Eschar Slough**
- hidden headquarters: **The Scab Cathedral**
- central market / council chamber: **The Throat**
- dry-season identity: bandits, salvage crews, convoy raiders, route intelligence dealers
- wet-season identity: pirates, black harbor operators, barge raiders, maritime smugglers
- motto candidate: **Nothing owns what the wound rejects.**

Canon principle:

**The Free Scabs are not the wound. They are what grew over the wound after every official map labeled it empty.**
