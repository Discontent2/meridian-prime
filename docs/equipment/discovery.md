# Discovery

> Canon reference for Discovery, Rudy Calder's mechanic / field-repair support rig in MITE II operations.

**Canon Status:** MAIN CANON, EXPANDABLE
**Canon Weight:** Vehicle / Equipment / Character-Linked Rig
**Scope:** Rudy Calder, MITE II convoy logistics, field repair, machine-truth pressure
**Source of Truth:** Yes for Rudy Calder as Discovery's operator

---

## Core Canon

**Discovery** is Rudy Calder's mechanic / field-repair support rig.

Rudy Calder is both:

- Discovery's primary operator / driver
- MITE II's lead mechanic and field-repair authority

Discovery is not Red Umbrielor.

Discovery is not a subsystem of Red Umbrielor.

Discovery should not be filed under Red Umbrielor except as part of shared MITE II convoy operations.

Clean distinction:

| Vehicle / System | Primary Association | Function |
|---|---|---|
| **409** | Ildi Vey / Kavo "Forest" Rill | lead sensor / terrain-reading tractor |
| **289 Red Umbrielor** | Tenet Nocsid III | command / life-support tractor |
| **287 Discovery** | Rudy Calder | mechanic / field-repair support rig |
| **MADS** | Red Umbrielor | onboard diagnostic / alert system |
| **MD-8** | fuel logistics | Meridian Distillate 8 fuel |

## Rudy Calder Relationship

Rudy drives Discovery because machine truth should have its own wheels.

Discovery is the place where Rudy can:

- monitor the Mod train
- audit couplings
- diagnose equipment mismatch
- carry tools, parts, coolant, field repair supplies, and emergency fix capacity
- complain about Red Umbrielor with authority
- identify when the MITE II loadout does not match the official mission
- keep the convoy alive when the packet lies

Rudy may know Red Umbrielor almost as well as Tenet does, but Discovery is his operational seat.

Canon line:

**409 reads the route. Tenet drives Red Umbrielor. Rudy drives Discovery. The argument between them is called maintenance.**

## Vehicle Identity

Discovery should feel like a working mechanic's rig, not a sleek hero vehicle.

Possible descriptors:

- blunt field-repair rig
- rolling tool chapel
- mechanic support hauler
- mobile parts shrine
- grease-stained field ambulance for dying machines
- tool lockers down both flanks
- bad welds with stories
- enough spare hose to frighten a procurement officer
- repair gantries, coupling monitors, and quick-access field lockers

Exact make, model, class, and drivetrain remain expandable unless later canon locks them.

Discovery can be treated as a vehicle, rig, hauler, or support unit depending on scene needs, but Rudy should be understood as **driving / operating** it during MITE II field movement.

## Chapter 2 Use

In Chapter 2, Rudy should enter from Discovery, not from a generic mechanic Mod.

Preferred entrance mode:

Rudy comes over comms from Discovery while driving / operating the support rig, complaining that Red Umbrielor's defrost is not broken, it is **"protesting management."**

Correct drafting direction:

> Rudy's voice came through from Discovery, thick with engine static and cheerful doom.

Avoid:

> Rudy appears from the mechanic Mod.

If a mechanic Mod exists in the MITE II train, Discovery may coordinate with it, tow it, support it, or carry related repair capacity, but Rudy's character anchor is Discovery.

Important choreography note:

Discovery is not the lead vehicle. **409** leads the moving Traverse as the sensor tractor. Red Umbrielor follows as command / life-support tractor. Discovery follows as repair / storage / sustainment tractor.

---

## Story Function

Discovery reinforces Rudy's role as **machine truth**.

If Rudy is worried, the equipment load is wrong.

If Discovery is overpacked, underused, or carrying repair capacity beyond the stated mission, the reader should feel the job's practical dishonesty before anyone names conspiracy.

Possible Rudy logic:

**A survey does not bring this much repair unless somebody expects the route to bite back.**

---

## Guardrails

- Do not make Discovery a duplicate of Red Umbrielor.
- Do not make Discovery the front sensor vehicle.
- Do not make Rudy merely a passenger in a mechanic Mod unless a specific scene requires him to leave Discovery temporarily.
- Do not make Discovery sleek, magical, or implausibly clean.
- Do not over-spec Discovery before story needs require it.
- Do not let Discovery replace Rudy's competence; the rig is his tool, not his personality.
- Do not let Discovery become comic relief by itself. The humor comes from Rudy's competence and fatalism.
