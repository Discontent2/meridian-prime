## Core Patch

Craton Geodeums include at least one recognized arena subtype built around heavy industrial machines.

These are not sleek military mechs by default.

Craton arena mechs generally descend from modified construction, quarry, loading, lifting, hauling, excavation, and heavy labor equipment. Their power comes from public industrial force, not separate military polish.

Craton society may carry a steampunk visual style without being technologically limited to primitive machinery. The style reflects cultural engineering language: exposed mechanisms, pressure systems, brass, iron, rivets, analog displays, visible linkages, ceremonial hardware, and machines that show their burden instead of hiding it.

Core line:

```text
Craton mechs are labor machines taught to stand trial in public.
```

Another:

```text
The war machine still remembers it was built to work.
```

## Geodeum Subtype: Mech Geodeum

A **Mech Geodeum** is a Craton Geodeum arena where modified heavy equipment, loader frames, quarry rigs, lift-rigs, haulers, or other industrial walkers are used in public contests, ceremonial ordeals, status disputes, or Stonebound spectacles.

A Mech Geodeum still follows the larger Geodeum logic:

```text
The crowd watches.
The stone listens.
The machine becomes part of the witness event.
```

A Mech Geodeum may function as:

```text
industrial arena
public machine contest chamber
Stonebound mech ring
quarry-frame contest site
loader-frame proving ground
Crown Stonebound spectacle arena
labor-machine ordeal space
```

## Dragulies's Mech: Stonejaw's Lift Rig

Dragulies is canonically associated with a famous loader-type Geodeum mech called:

```text
Stonejaw's Lift Rig
```

Stonejaw's Lift Rig is a heavy loader-frame arena mech with Craton steampunk industrial styling. It descends from construction / stone-yard heavy equipment rather than purpose-built military machinery.

Its base identity:

```text
loader-type mech
modified heavy labor frame
Craton Geodeum arena machine
Crown Stonebound machine legacy
steampunk industrial styling
stone-yard / quarry / lifting lineage
```

The machine was acquired by Dragulies from the previous Crown holder, whose name was **Stonejaw**.

This makes the machine a public inheritance, a trophy, and a burden. Dragulies did not merely gain a mech. He gained the weight of the prior Crown holder's name in metal, pressure, and stone dust.

Core line:

```text
Stonejaw's Lift Rig is not only Dragulies's machine. It is the previous Crown holder refusing to leave the arena quietly.
```

## Relationship Between Dragulies and the Rig

Dragulies remains famous as a Tyranosapien Crown Stonebound in his own right. Stonejaw's Lift Rig does not replace his body, reputation, refusal, or witness role.

The rig extends his public meaning into the Mech Geodeum tradition.

On foot, Dragulies is the body under witness.

In Stonejaw's Lift Rig, Dragulies becomes the operator of inherited industrial consequence.

Short form:

```text
Dragulies is the witness with teeth.
Stonejaw's Lift Rig is the burden with arms.
```
