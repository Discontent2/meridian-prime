# Meridian Prime Timeline

> Fictional worldbuilding reference for the Meridian Prime universe.

**Dating convention:** This timeline uses **A0** as the arrival of **The Lodestar** at Meridian Prime / Kepler-1649c. Dates before arrival are marked **A-**. Dates after arrival are marked **A+**.

This document is a living continuity skeleton. It records confirmed canon, provisional sequence logic, public history, hidden history, and open timeline questions for future novels, comics, games, and reference material.

---

## Timeline Track System

Each major timeline event may be described through multiple tracks. This keeps official history, hidden history, and native-world history from collapsing into one flat account.

### Tracks

**Public History**
What ordinary Meridians are taught or allowed to believe.

**World Works / Hidden History**
What World Works, The Lodestar archives, or other concealed records may reveal.

**Aeonos / Aeonolacertian History**
How the event affects or is remembered by the Aeonolacertians of Matter Aeonos.

**àæonos / áæbsconditian History**
How the event affects or is remembered by the áæbsconditians of anti-matter àæonos.

### Canon Status Labels

**CONFIRMED CANON**
Locked setting fact.

**PROVISIONAL CANON**
Accepted for now, but open to refinement.

**PUBLIC VERSION**
The official or common explanation within Meridian society.

**HIDDEN VERSION**
The deeper truth, suppressed record, or buried interpretation.

**OPEN QUESTION**
Unresolved timeline point that should remain available for future story development.

## Pre-Human Aeonos

### Deep Pre-Human Era: Aeonos and àæonos Exist as Entangled Worlds

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- **Aeonos** exists as the matter-world later known to humans as Meridian Prime.
- **àæonos** exists as the anti-matter quantum-entangled counterpart to Aeonos.
- The two worlds are linked by quantum entanglement, resonance, memory bleed, and continuity distortion.
- They are **not exact mirrors**. Each world possesses its own geography, ecosystems, history, and civilization.

### Rise of the Aeonolacertians

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- The **Aeonolacertians** emerge as the native civilization of Matter Aeonos.
- They are a crossbred saurian species whose social classes express different saurian trait-lines:
  - Velociraptoroid
  - Tyrannisauroid
  - Bajadasauroid
  - Ankylosauroid
  - Ceratopsinoid
- Their technology appears primitive and steampunk-like, but is actually extremely advanced.

### Rise of the áæbsconditians

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- The **áæbsconditians**, commonly simplified in human records as **Absconditians**, emerge as the native civilization of anti-matter àæonos.
- They are blue-skinned humanoids with black irises, magenta pupils, and teal tapetum lucidum.
- They are not elves, orcs, zombies, or undead. They are their own anti-matter civilization.
- Their technology is cyberpunk in aesthetic and extremely advanced.
- The word **áæbsconditia** is difficult for human speech and is often approximated as **AIB-skon-DISH-ee-uh**.

### Pre-Contact Civilizational Development

**Canon Status:** PROVISIONAL CANON

- Aeonolacertians and áæbsconditians develop independently but remain bound by the entanglement between Aeonos and àæonos.
- The exact nature of contact, awareness, conflict, trade, observation, or contamination between the two worlds remains undefined.
- Both civilizations reach heights of technology and culture before human interference.

## Project Meridian Prime

### Pre-Launch Human Era

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- Humans identify **Kepler-1649c**, the third planet in the Meridia system, as a colonization target.
- A single-shot, one-time generational colonization mission is developed.
- The mission is named **Project Meridian Prime**.
- The generation ship is named **The Lodestar**.

### Mission Design

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- Project Meridian Prime is built around one ship, one trajectory, and one chance of success.
- There is no second colony fleet.
- There is no guaranteed rescue wave.
- The mission requires the shipborn population to survive, govern, reproduce, educate, and maintain civilization across a 200-year interstellar journey.

### First Traverse Leader

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- **Ernest Skelton** becomes the first Traverse Leader.
- The Traverse Leader serves as the highest executive, civic, navigational, and symbolic authority aboard The Lodestar.

## The Traverse

### A-200: Departure of The Lodestar

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- The Lodestar departs human space on Project Meridian Prime.
- The 200-year interstellar journey begins.
- The mission destination is still technically Kepler-1649c.
- The name Meridian Prime initially refers to the project, not the planet itself.

### Early Traverse

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- First shipborn generations grow up aboard The Lodestar.
- Earth begins shifting from lived reality into inherited memory.
- The mission name **Meridian Prime** begins accumulating emotional and cultural meaning.
- Children born aboard the ship imagine Meridian Prime as the promised world they have never seen.

### Mid-Traverse

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- Earth becomes history, then legend, then argument.
- Multiple Traverse Leaders rise and pass.
- Shipborn law, ritual, education, and social structure evolve inside The Lodestar.
- Meridian Prime becomes not merely a destination, but a civic myth.

### Late Traverse

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- The Lodestar-born population increasingly treats Meridian Prime as the name of the planet itself.
- Kepler-1649c remains a scientific or archival designation.
- Meridian Prime becomes the cultural homeland before humanity ever lands there.

## A-30 Crisis Chain

The A-30 Crisis Chain is the major pre-arrival event cluster connecting the Poisoned Contact, the Surface Insurgency, the Catastrophic Interaction, and the Auto-Coup. It is the hinge where the native-world catastrophe and the human political takeover become one event-web.

### A-31 to A-30: Anomaly Detection

**Canon Status:** PROVISIONAL CANON

**Public History**
No public version exists, or the records are folded into later accounts of the Late Traverse Emergency.

**World Works / Hidden History**
Select humans aboard or connected to The Lodestar detect evidence that Kepler-1649c is not an ordinary colony target. The existence of Aeonos, àæonos, or their entangled civilizations becomes known to a restricted group.

**Aeonos / Aeonolacertian History**
Aeonolacertians may detect unusual off-world observation or strange signal behavior before recognizing human involvement.

**àæonos / áæbsconditian History**
áæbsconditians may detect signal disturbance, resonance irregularity, or foreign observation through anti-matter systems.

### A-30: First Covert Contact

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

**Public History**
No official public record acknowledges pre-arrival contact with native civilizations.

**World Works / Hidden History**
Select humans establish hidden contact with the Aeonolacertians of Aeonos and the áæbsconditians of àæonos before The Lodestar arrives.

**Aeonos / Aeonolacertian History**
Aeonolacertians receive contact from unknown or poorly understood human intermediaries.

**àæonos / áæbsconditian History**
áæbsconditians receive contact from unknown or poorly understood human intermediaries.

### A-30: The Poisoned Contact

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

**Public History**
The public later hears only that a pre-arrival instability threatened mission continuity.

**World Works / Hidden History**
The contact corrupts the flow of knowledge between Aeonos and àæonos. Each civilization receives misleading or distorted information about the other, increasing fear, mistrust, and escalation.

**Aeonos / Aeonolacertian History**
Aeonolacertian factions interpret the distorted information as evidence of severe áæbsconditian threat.

**àæonos / áæbsconditian History**
áæbsconditian factions interpret the distorted information as evidence of severe Aeonolacertian threat.

### A-30: The False Parley

**Canon Status:** PROVISIONAL CANON

**Public History**
No public version exists. The False Parley is unknown to ordinary Meridians.

**World Works / Hidden History**
Human agents or systems present themselves as intermediaries, observers, messengers, allies, or witnesses while shaping what each side believes about the other.

**Aeonos / Aeonolacertian History**
Some Aeonolacertians may remember the False Parley as a failed diplomatic channel or sacred betrayal.

**àæonos / áæbsconditian History**
Some áæbsconditians may remember the False Parley as proof that matter-side diplomacy cannot be trusted.

### A-30: Surface Insurgency Begins

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

**Public History**
Human records describe a Surface Insurgency connected to Meridian Prime’s surface. The details are vague, sanitized, or contradictory.

**World Works / Hidden History**
The Surface Insurgency is an engineered native-world crisis created by the information corruption of the Poisoned Contact. It provides the atmosphere of emergency needed for the Auto-Coup.

**Aeonos / Aeonolacertian History**
Aeonolacertian factions fracture under fear, urgency, and competing interpretations of the crisis.

**àæonos / áæbsconditian History**
áæbsconditian operations intensify in response to perceived existential threat.

### A-30: Emergency Powers Invoked

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

**Public History**
The Late Traverse Emergency is remembered as a necessary response to instability near the destination.

**World Works / Hidden History**
Elements within mission authority use the Surface Insurgency to justify expanded emergency powers aboard The Lodestar.

**Aeonos / Aeonolacertian History**
Aeonolacertians may not understand that the crisis on Aeonos is being used as political leverage aboard The Lodestar.

**àæonos / áæbsconditian History**
áæbsconditians may not understand that their reactions are helping consolidate human authority off-world.

### A-30: The Auto-Coup

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

**Public History**
Later public language may call this period the Continuity Crisis, Stabilization Period, Late Traverse Emergency, Final Alignment, or Pre-Arrival Security Mandate.

**World Works / Hidden History**
Lawful mission authority is converted into controlled authority. The cabal that becomes World Works gains the structural power needed to inherit Meridian Prime on arrival.

**Aeonos / Aeonolacertian History**
The Auto-Coup is largely invisible to Aeonolacertians, though its consequences shape the human arrival that follows.

**àæonos / áæbsconditian History**
The Auto-Coup is largely invisible to áæbsconditians, though its consequences shape human control of the matter-world after arrival.

### A-30: The Catastrophic Interaction / Thousandfold Rupture

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

**Public History**
If known at all, the event is described through sterile terms such as the Pre-Contact Anomaly or treated as an ancient mystery.

**World Works / Hidden History**
The Surface Insurgency catalyzes the Catastrophic Interaction between Matter Aeonos and anti-matter àæonos. The resulting disaster leaves both native civilizations severely diminished before The Lodestar’s arrival.

**Aeonos / Aeonolacertian History**
The event may be remembered as the Sundering of Scales. Aeonolacertian population falls to approximately 1,000 survivors.

**àæonos / áæbsconditian History**
The event may be remembered as the Failed Unmaking. áæbsconditian population falls to approximately 1,000 survivors.

### A-29: Survivor Suppression and Record Curation

**Canon Status:** PROVISIONAL CANON

**Public History**
The public receives only simplified accounts of mission preservation, crisis response, and continuity management.

**World Works / Hidden History**
Records are curated to conceal human responsibility for the Poisoned Contact, Surface Insurgency, and Catastrophic Interaction. The Inheritance Protocol becomes buried beneath emergency doctrine.

**Aeonos / Aeonolacertian History**
Aeonolacertian survivors retreat, conceal themselves, or reorganize around survival memory.

**àæonos / áæbsconditian History**
áæbsconditian survivors retreat into stealth, secrecy, or retaliatory long-game planning.

### A0: Arrival Under World Works Continuity

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

**Public History**
The Lodestar arrives at the promised world: Meridian Prime.

**World Works / Hidden History**
The colony arrives under an already-compromised continuity regime. World Works has positioned itself to control settlement, records, archives, and the public story.

**Aeonos / Aeonolacertian History**
Aeonolacertians witness human arrival after catastrophic population collapse.

**àæonos / áæbsconditian History**
áæbsconditians witness or infer human arrival as the final proof that the earlier crisis served human benefit.

## The World Works Conspiracy

### A-30: Emergence of the World Works Cabal

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- Approximately 30 years before arrival, the hidden cabal that will become **World Works Corporation** begins active operations.
- The cabal uses subversion, conspiracy, false diplomacy, and destabilization to position itself to inherit the colony’s future.
- Its hidden doctrine may be known as **The Inheritance Protocol**.

### A-30: Two-Front Operation

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

World Works operates on two connected fronts:

1. **The Poisoned Contact**: covert human interference in the relationship between Aeonos and àæonos.
2. **The Auto-Coup**: the conversion of mission authority aboard The Lodestar into controlled authority.

### Birth of World Works Power

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- World Works does not conquer Meridian Prime after arrival.
- It arranges to inherit Meridian Prime before arrival.
- The phrase **Control over Continuity** begins as doctrine and eventually becomes civic culture.

## Arrival Era

### A0: Arrival at Kepler-1649c / Meridian Prime

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- The Lodestar reaches Kepler-1649c after the 200-year Traverse.
- By this point, the human population largely knows the destination as **Meridian Prime**.
- The project name becomes the human name for the planet.
- The arriving colony enters a world whose native civilizations have already been reduced to survivor populations.

### First Human Establishment on Meridian Prime

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- Humans establish a permanent presence on the surface.
- **Prime Ops Space Port** eventually becomes the key authorized transit point between the surface and The Lodestar.
- The original well-built Lodestar shuttles are used for surface-orbit travel.

### The Lodestar Remains in Orbit

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- The Lodestar is not dismantled after arrival.
- It remains active in orbit above Meridian Prime.
- Over time, it becomes the corporate headquarters of World Works Corporation.
- From the surface, it appears as a very large moving artificial star with red blinking lights.

## Corporate Meridian Era

### Consolidation of World Works

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- World Works becomes the dominant corporate and political force tied to The Lodestar.
- It controls or heavily influences orbital infrastructure, public history, archives, transportation, security, and civic continuity.
- The general public knows what they are supposed to know.

### Development of the Public Creed

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- **Control over Continuity** becomes a common public saying.
- Among ordinary Meridians, it means: **Everything will work out the way it is supposed to.**
- To World Works, it means the active management of history, memory, governance, archives, education, perception, and public belief.
- Hidden beneath the creed is the need to protect a founding lie.

### Technological Divergence

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- Surface human technology degrades into a dieselpunk condition.
- The Lodestar and its inhabitants exploit a solarpunk aesthetic to project harmony, purity, and legitimacy.
- Aeonolacertian technology remains primitive-steampunk in appearance but extremely advanced in function.
- áæbsconditian technology remains cyberpunk in aesthetic and extremely advanced.

### Faction Strength Balance

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- World Works has the strongest military power in numbers.
- Aeonolacertians are strongest in brute strength.
- áæbsconditians are strongest in stealth.
- Meridians are strongest in willpower.

## Present Day

### The World Below

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- The surface population of Meridian Prime lives under degraded dieselpunk conditions.
- Meridians endure corporate control, corrupted planetary memory, curated public truth, and life beneath The Lodestar’s red blinking presence.
- Their defining strength is willpower.

### The Ark Above

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- The Lodestar remains active in orbit.
- It serves as World Works corporate headquarters.
- It is visible from the surface as a large moving star with red blinking lights.
- It functions as relic, headquarters, orbital citadel, archive, and symbol of controlled continuity.

### The Hidden Older War

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- Aeonolacertians remain tied to Matter Aeonos and the aftermath of the Catastrophic Interaction.
- áæbsconditians remain tied to anti-matter àæonos and the aftermath of the Catastrophic Interaction.
- The consequences of the Catastrophic Interaction continue to shape planetary memory, continuity, and conflict.

### The Civic Lie

**Canon Status:** CONFIRMED CANON

- The public knows the official story of Meridian Prime.
- The deeper truth of World Works, The Poisoned Contact, The False Parley, The Auto-Coup, the Surface Insurgency, Aeonos, àæonos, and the Catastrophic Interaction remains dangerous.
- The central present-day tension is whether continuity can survive truth.
