# Meridian Prime Ecology Anchor Index

> Curated index of first-pass Meridian Prime ecology anchors promoted from sandbox development into main canon at name / role level.

**Canon Status:** MAIN CANON INDEX, EXPANDABLE

## Purpose

This index canonizes a curated starter set of major ecological anchors without promoting every sandbox detail.

These names exist in main canon as regional ecological anchors. Their exact biology, behavior, cultural meaning, and story use remain expandable.

Use this file as a map of what ecological names are available, not as a final bestiary, botany guide, or pharmacopoeia.

Core guardrail:

**Promote the anchor. Expand the organism only when story need demands it.**

## Black-Market / Law-Relevant Ecology Seeds

The following ecology-linked legal concepts are strong candidate canon and may be developed in politics files:

- **Weight Theft**: Craton term for sacred fauna contraband that steals not merely tissue, but the animal's place in pressure, route, memory, and sacred land.
- **Civic-Origin Violation**: Hydropolis protected material, signal ecology, or verified-origin breach.
- **Route-Prohibited Substance**: any food, medicine, intoxicant, or ritual substance banned before descent, convoy work, docking, or technical route duty.
- **Toxic Lookalike Hazard**: foodlike ecology that becomes dangerous because it resembles a staple or safe harvest.
- **Verified-Origin Food / Medicine**: resource whose safety depends on who named, harvested, certified, or witnessed it.

- `docs/culture/food-culture-and-sovereignty.md`

## Guardrails

- Do not promote the entire sandbox ecology catalogue into canon at once.
- Do not treat every listed anchor as fully detailed final canon.
- Do not create real-world medical or toxicological instructions.
- Do not make every named organism appear in Book One.
- Do not use ecology as random flavor if it does not affect route, law, food, map, belief, or survival.
- Do not turn Craton, Hydropolis, or human settlement ecology into interchangeable biomes.
