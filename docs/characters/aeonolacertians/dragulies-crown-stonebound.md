## Core Identity

Dragulies is a famous Tyranosapien Stonebound whose victories made him a public symbol before anyone agreed what he symbolized.

He is known across Craton and Craton-adjacent circles as **Dragulies, Crown Stonebound**, and in crowd speech as **the Verdict-Breaker**.

He is not merely a famous fighter. He is a person whose Geodeum record became social material: quoted by crowds, feared by officials, claimed by factions, misused by opportunists, and remembered by those who survived public judgment.

Core line:

```text
Dragulies does not fight to prove he is strong. Everyone already knows that. He fights to make the stone admit what the crowd saw.
```

Shorter line:

```text
Dragulies is not a monster in the arena. He is the arena learning it has witnesses.
```

## Stonebound Terminology

Within Meridian Prime, **gladiator** is an outside translation, not the best in-world term.

The stronger in-world category is:

```text
Stonebound
```

A **Stonebound** is a person who stands in a Geodeum under witness. Depending on the Geodeum and the event, a Stonebound may fight, endure, testify, represent a claim, answer an accusation, or survive an ordeal.

Related terms:

```text
Stonebound: broad formal term for Geodeum ordeal / contest participants.
Ringjaw: rough crowd slang for famous arena Stonebound.
Verdict Champion: formal title for an elite Stonebound acting as a recognized proxy in a dispute.
Stone-Witness: post-ordeal title or description for someone who survives a Judgment Geodeum and carries public consequence afterward.
```

Dragulies is a Crown Stonebound: an elite Stonebound associated with high-status, formal, or politically visible Geodeum contests.

**Dragulies** is his fighter name and public stone-name.

His private birth name is not defined by this entry. It may be withheld, lost beneath public fame, or deliberately protected by custom.

The name Dragulies should feel chantable, carvable, marketable, and difficult to take back once spoken by a crowd.

## Tyranosapien Physical Presence

Dragulies is a Tyranosapien / Tyrannosauroid Aeonolacertian.

He should not be depicted as a human with reptile traits, a generic lizardfolk warrior, or a dinosaur monster.

He is an intelligent, culturally situated, fully saurian person with a mostly upright tool-using body plan and Tyranosapien expression.

Visual language:

```text
massive upright saurian body
deep chest
powerful neck and jaw
heavy counterbalancing tail
thick brow ridge
large tyrannosauroid skull and snout
shorter but strong forearms adapted to his morphology
four-fingered clawed hands unless later class-specific anatomy revises this
four-toed clawed feet with arena-worn traction scars
old impact scars across muzzle, chest, shoulder plates, and tail base
ritual stone dust caught in scale seams
Geodeum mark cords or Crown Stonebound ornaments fitted around non-human anatomy
```

Mandatory visual guardrail:

```text
Do not depict Dragulies as human with scales. Do not use a human face. Do not use normal human hands or feet. Do not omit the tail. Do not make him generic fantasy lizardfolk. Do not make him a mindless dinosaur beast. His body must read as Tyranosapien first, humanoid tool-user second.
```

## Fighting Presence

Dragulies is not quick first.

He is inevitable.

His Geodeum style is based on pressure, positioning, witness control, refusal, and making the arena smaller by occupying it correctly.

Story-facing movement language:

```text
slow ring control
low bracing stance
heavy tail counterbalance
jaw feints that force the crowd to breathe in at once
shoulder pressure against stone boundaries
floor-testing stomps that wake mineral resonance
turning lanes closed by body placement
refusing to be moved when the verdict expects him to fall
```

Do not write real-world combat instructions. Keep arena action cinematic, symbolic, and story-facing.

## Fame

Dragulies became famous not simply because he won.

He became famous because he changed what his wins meant.

Public stories say he survived a contest he was supposed to lose, and later survived a Judgment Geodeum ordeal that should have made him socially disposable. The exact legal history, sponsoring faction, and formal precedent are left for later controlled development.

What is canon here:

```text
Dragulies is a famous Tyranosapien Crown Stonebound.
He is associated with Craton Geodeums.
He is known as the Verdict-Breaker.
His fame involves both arena spectacle and judgment pressure.
He is publicly remembered for refusing clean outcomes imposed by others.
```

## Moral Shape

Dragulies is famous for physical force, but his deeper power is refusal.

Not pacifism.

Refusal.

He refuses bad verdicts. He refuses false surrender. He refuses to let officials pretend the crowd did not see something. He refuses to be reduced to a beast, emblem, mascot, punishment tool, or convenient precedent.

He may be admired as a champion, feared as a dangerous public memory, resented as a legal embarrassment, or claimed by factions who do not understand him.

Core contradiction:

```text
The crowd wanted a fighter. The stone got a witness with teeth.
```
